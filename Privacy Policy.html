Shifting Pillars Privacy Policy

Who we are

The app Shifting Pillars is developed and maintained by Banaworks, a German company that develops games for mobile devices and desktop computers. If you don't find us online yet, don't worry, we are still working on building our online presence.

Which of your data we use

This app does not access, collect, use, or share any sensitive or personal identifiable user data.

The only data collected is the game progress. This data is stored locally on your device and deleted when the app is uninstalled.

How long we store your data

Your game progress is stored on your device as long as you have the app installed.

How to delete your data

You can delete your game progress by uninstalling the app.
